<h2>OGNL 表达式</h2>
<p>OGNL(Object Graphic Navigation Language, 对象图导航语言)<br>
<img src="9_26_01.PNG" alt="alt"></p>
<ol>
<li>方法参数是String 或基本数据类型(包装器类型)<br>
<code><span class="hljs-keyword">select</span>(<span class="hljs-built_in">String</span> userName)     <span class="hljs-meta">#{userName}</span></code></li>
<li>参数是JavaBean对象类型<br>
<code>add(<span class="hljs-keyword">User</span> <span class="hljs-title">user</span>)     <span class="hljs-comment">#{属性名} #{userId} #{password}</span></code></li>
<li>参数是Map的时候<br>
<code>add(<span class="hljs-built_in">Map</span>&lt;<span class="hljs-built_in">String</span>, <span class="hljs-built_in">Object</span>&gt;)     <span class="hljs-comment">#{key} #{userId}</span></code></li>
<li>多参数1<br>
<code><span class="hljs-selector-tag">queryList</span>(<span class="hljs-variable">@Param</span>(<span class="hljs-string">"a"</span>)String userId, <span class="hljs-variable">@Param</span>(<span class="hljs-string">"b"</span>)String userName)     #{<span class="hljs-selector-tag">a</span>} #{<span class="hljs-selector-tag">b</span>}</code></li>
<li>多参数2<br>
<code><span class="hljs-selector-tag">list</span>(<span class="hljs-variable">@Param</span>(<span class="hljs-string">"u"</span>) User user, <span class="hljs-variable">@Param</span>(<span class="hljs-string">"d"</span>)String deptId)     #{<span class="hljs-selector-tag">u</span><span class="hljs-selector-class">.userId</span>} #{<span class="hljs-selector-tag">u</span><span class="hljs-selector-class">.userName</span>}    #{<span class="hljs-selector-tag">d</span>}</code></li>
</ol>
<p><strong>注意</strong> 在xml中，如果参数是字符串类型，如<code><span class="hljs-meta">#{userName}===&gt;<span class="hljs-string">'张三'</span></span></code> (自动加单引号), 写 <code><span class="hljs-variable">${userName}</span>====&gt;张三</code> 没有单引号</p>
<h2>resultMap 和 resultType的区别</h2>
<p>二者不可共存</p>
<p><code><span class="hljs-attribute">resultType</span></code>: 方法返回值是具体的类型,数据库表的【列名】映射到返回值上，如果返回值是JavaBean, 那么返回值映射关系为：列名--&gt;属性名<br>
<code><span class="hljs-attribute">resultMap</span></code>: 自定义的映射关系</p>
