<h2 id="配置系统服务"><a href="#配置系统服务">配置系统服务</a></h2>
<p>新建 <code>/lib/systemd/system/aria2.service</code>，配置如下</p>
<pre><code class="hljs language-conf">Description= aria2
After=network.target
 
[Service]
User=smile
Group=smile

#PIDFile=/run/aria2.pid
ExecStart=aria2c -c --conf-path=/home/smile/.aria2/aria2.conf &#x26;
ExecStop=/bin/kill $MAINPID
RestartSec=always
 
[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
</code></pre>
